## ✨ What Was Built

A comprehensive **Patient Wellness App MVP** featuring:

### Core Features:
1. **📊 Dashboard**
   - Real-time health stats (steps, heart rate, sleep, water)
   - Smart health alerts
   - Quick action buttons

2. **💓 Health Metrics Tracker**
   - Log vital signs (BP, glucose, weight, temperature)
   - Historical data view
   - AI pattern detection

3. **💊 Medication Management**
   - Digital medication schedule
   - Smart reminders based on routines
   - Adherence tracking

4. **📋 Personalized Wellness Plan**
   - Customized daily activities
   - Progress tracking with visual charts
   - AI-powered recommendations for exercise, diet, and sleep
